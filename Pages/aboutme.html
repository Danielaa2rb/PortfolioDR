A little about me...

My name is Daniela Rocha, I live in Colombia, and I'm a UX/UI Developer. I like to stay in constantly learning, and I enjoy meeting people unafraid of sharing their ideas with the world.


Professionally, I like to create digital products and experiences that are true to their goals, always focusing on the user's needs. I am comfortable working on all aspects of the product cycle, from ideation workshops and research to prototyping journeys, crafting beautiful UIs.

  • Creation and optimization
  • Usability and Accessibility heuristic
  • Wireframing and iteration
  • Low and high fidelity prototyping
  • Interaction and motion design
  • Digital branding and style guides
  • User research and testing
